Reasons why I admire him:

  1. He is really creative - he made his house fly using thousands of balloons.
  2. He is loving - he cares really deeply about the people he loves, that he couldn't forget and let go of his wife after she passes away for years.
  3. He learns. When he knows that he has to let go in order to make his wife and himself happy, he is willing to really forgive and let his house stay on the waterfall in South America.
